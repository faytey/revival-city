About Our Church

Powerhouse Revival Communion is an Apostolic Revival Church Ministry, charged with the commission of reaching the world and empowering people for dominion by the Word of Faith.

God has given us a mandate to raise role models, and He has indeed been faithful, backing our dedication and hard work with a great harvest of transformed lives. It is top priority for us that you partake of wholesome worship, edifying messages to build you up and positive interactions. We invite you to tour our website and get to know us a bit better - our service times, locations and membership. There are also links should you wish to support our ministry by giving. We look forward to having the pleasure of hosting you and your family soon, either in person at one of our branches or virtually.

In Christ, Dr. & Mrs. Roberts Onoja.
